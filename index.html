Est Est Est is the ultimate restaurant for fans of Italian, Pizza, Mediterranean, European, Romana, Lazio, and Central Italian cuisine. In our restaurant, you will experience the authentic Italian flavors alongside a selection of meals to suit every palate.

Special Recipes

Taste of Precious

Hearty meal Authentic and delicious dishes At Est Est Est, we think that delicious cooking begins with high-quality, fresh ingredients. Because of this, we get our ingredients from regional vendors and meticulously create each meal. Our menu includes something for everyone, whether you're craving a classic Margherita pizza, a filling pasta meal, or a refreshing Mediterranean salad.

Cannelloni agli spinaci

Cannelloni is a baked Italian dish of large pasta tubes filled with a variety of ingredients such as cheese, spinach, or meat, and smothered in a tomato or Bechamel sauce.

Pasta Carbonara

Pasta Carbonara is a classic Italian dish made with spaghetti, eggs, Parmesan cheese, and pancetta or bacon. It has a rich and creamy flavor with a perfect balance of salty and savory notes.

Grilled Gamberoni

Grilled gamberoni is a flavorful and succulent Italian dish consisting of large grilled prawns seasoned with olive oil, garlic, lemon, and herbs.

Linguine all'Astice

Linguine all'Astice is a delicious Italian dish made with linguine pasta and fresh lobster, cooked in a tomato-based sauce with garlic, white wine, and herbs.

Risotto Agli Scampi

Risotto agli scampi is a classic Italian dish made with creamy risotto cooked with fresh scampi, white wine, butter, Parmesan cheese, and aromatic herbs.

Vitello Tonnato

Vitello tonnato is a popular Italian dish that consists of thin slices of tender veal smothered in a creamy sauce made from tuna, anchovies, mayonnaise, capers, lemon juice, and olive oil.
